How this module works

{% if user.role == 'lgu_admin' %} Analytics for your LGU combines approved tourist spots, tourist feedback, and arrival reports submitted by establishments you manage. Visitor origin and gender data come from the arrival_reports table. {% elif user.role == 'establishment_owner' %} Track visitor submissions and ratings for your property. Daily and weekly counts flow to your LGU; they compile monthly totals for LTCATO. Feedback and engagement data update in real time. {% elif user.role == 'ltcato_staff' %} Provincial view across all LGUs, establishments, and monthly submissions. Arrivals, feedback sentiment, and event analytics are aggregated from Supabase in real time. {% else %} System-wide KPIs: approved destinations, pending approvals, monthly arrivals, feedback sentiment, tourist engagement, itineraries, and passport stamps across Laguna province. {% endif %}

How Decision Support Works

1. Data Collection

Scrapers pull weather, Laguna tourism news, Google Trends, and spot reviews from free public sources.

2. Sentiment Analysis

TextBlob labels every feedback and review as positive, neutral, or negative.

3. Signal Aggregation

Weather alerts, sentiment scores, trending keywords, and news tone combined into one view.

4. Recommendations

Rule-based engine generates color-coded priority cards for LTCATO staff to act on.

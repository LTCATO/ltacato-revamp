Who we are

Serving Laguna's visitors, culture, and commerce

LTCATO is the provincial office mandated to develop and promote Laguna as a premier tourism, cultural, and trade destination. We work with local government units, tourism officers, cultural workers, businesses, and the public to deliver programs that meet national standards and community needs.

This digital platform helps travelers discover verified tourist spots, plan itineraries, explore events, and access transparent information about our frontline services — including the full Citizen's Charter published below.

Discover Laguna

The Pearl of the Orient

Laguna is home to storied towns, thermal resorts, waterfalls, pilgrimage sites, culinary traditions, and festivals that draw visitors year-round. From Calamba's heritage to Pagsanjan's rapids, Lumban's embroidery, and Paete's woodcarving — each municipality contributes to a diverse provincial identity.

  • Lakes, hot springs, and natural parks
  • Heritage churches and historical landmarks
  • Festivals, food fairs, and cultural events
  • Warm hospitality and community-led tourism
